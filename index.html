Adam Avery

contact@adamaverysongs.com

Adam is an award-winning, ASCAP songwriter who has a knack for crafting strong melodies with infectious hooks. He earned his first cut in 2012 when his song Redefined, was recorded by Chicago based band, Daniel’s Window. Adam lives with his wife and four children in the Green Mountains of Burlington, Vermont.
